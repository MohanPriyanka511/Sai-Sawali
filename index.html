A Home of Hope and Opportunity for Every Child

We are Sai Sawali Baal Pariwaar

At Sai Sawali Baal Pariwar, we nurture, educate, and empower orphaned children to build a bright and independent future.

"Our Mission"

We strive to provide a loving and supportive environment for children in need, offering them education, healthcare, and the emotional support needed to thrive. Sai Sawali Baal Pariwar is a safe haven where every child is valued and empowered.

"Our Impact"

With the support of our community, we have been able to make a meaningful difference in the lives of countless children.

  • 200+ children educated and supported
  • 100% access to healthcare and wellness
  • 50+ dedicated volunteers contributing their time

"Our Programs"

  • Providing quality education to build a strong foundation.
  • Ensuring each child receives essential healthcare.
  • Empowering children with skills for a successful future.
